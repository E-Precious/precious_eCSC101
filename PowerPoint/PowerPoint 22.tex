	\subsection{History and Founder}
	Ruby was released in 1995 by Yukihiro "Matz" Matsumoto in Japan
	Yukihiro "Matz" Matsumoto was born on the 14th of April 1965 in Osaka, Japan. He is a software programmer and computer scientist who is mainly known for his work on Ruby.He works at Heroku where he developed Ruby, is a fellow at Rakuten Institute of Technology and is a technical advisor in Vasily.
	\subsection{Ruby}
	Ruby which is usually confused as Ruby on Rails but isnt Ruby on Rails  is object-oriented. It is described as terse, dynamic and it uses duck-typing. It allows for code-embedding and is a scripting language. It is high level and supports multiple programming paradigms.One major aspect of the language is that it focuses on simplicity.
